Partners

The Oshkosh Area Community Foundation collaborates with different partners to encourage giving and make a meaningful, long-term difference in local communities. By working with regional groups and dedicated funds, they help support specific local needs while also providing guidance, leadership, and efficient systems to make everything run smoothly. Since 2013 for example, the Winneconne Area Community Foundation supports nonprofit organizations serving residents in the Winneconne Community School District. (Source: Our Partners)

Events

The Oshkosh Area Community Foundation runs programs and events that bring people together and support local communities. For example, Tech-Free Tuesdays gives people fun, screen-free activities to enjoy, and Celebrate Education honors teachers, volunteers, and special school programs, giving out grants to help them continue their work. They also partner with groups like the Women’s Fund to provide sessions on helping kids use technology in healthy ways and supporting families and youth. The foundation also helps nonprofits work smarter and connect with each other. Workshops like AI Tools for Grant Writing show how to save time and reduce stress, while events like the Nonprofit Roundtable let organizations share ideas and collaborate. Local affiliates, like the Winneconne Area Community Foundation, celebrate achievements and show how the foundation supports long-term growth and positive impact in the region. (Source: Events)
