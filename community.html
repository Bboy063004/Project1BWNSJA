Partners

The Oshkosh Area Community Foundation collaborates with different partners to encourage giving and make a meaningful, long-term difference in local communities. By working with regional groups and dedicated funds, they help support specific local needs while also providing guidance, leadership, and efficient systems to make everything run smoothly.
